Paragraphs for writing task 1

  1. Introduction

    how to write an introduction

    1.compare

    2.give information about

    3.illustrates

    E. g. 原题干:

    The graph below shows the enrollments of overseas students and local students in Britain’s universities.

    Introduction:

    graph – table

    show – compare

    and – as well as

    Britain – UK

    The table compares （the foreign students as well as local students） who have enrolled in UK’s universities.

    E. g. 原题干:

    The graph below shows the proportion of the population aged 65 and over between 1940 and 2040 in the three different countries.

    Paraphrase:

    table — line

    proportion — percentage

    population — people

    aged 65 and over — aged 65 or more

    1940 and 2040 — over a period of years

    three different countries — namely

    The line graph compares the percentage of people aged 65 or more in three countries over a period of 100 years, namely, the UK, the US and China.
